# NHS Antidepressant Prescribing Analysis
## Project Overview
Analysis of real antidepressant prescribing data from the NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) Prescription Cost Analysis dataset, covering 32 drugs, 7 NHS England regions, and 46 months of national prescribing activity.
## Key Findings
- Sertraline hydrochloride is the highest-volume antidepressant prescribed nationally but has a relatively low cost-per-item
- Mirtazapine has a significantly higher cost-per-item, contributing disproportionately to total expenditure despite lower volume
- Statistically significant seasonal variation found in Sertraline and Mirtazapine prescribing — with implications for pharmaceutical supply chain planning
- Regional cost breakdowns reveal prescribing cost variation across 7 NHS England regions
